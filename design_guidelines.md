# Ambivers Landing Page - Design Guidelines

## Brand Overview
Ambivers is an educational platform helping Indonesian students prepare for university entrance exams with a modern, vibrant, and Gen Z-friendly aesthetic.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Yellow**: #FFC107 - Main brand color, used for CTAs and highlights (like "VERS" in logo)
- **Primary Blue**: #5B9BD5 - Secondary brand color, used for accents and ".ID" in logo
- **Accent Red**: #C00000 - Used sparingly for emphasis and important elements

### Neutral Colors
- **Background**: Clean white/light gray for main background
- **Text**: Dark gray/black for readability
- **Muted**: Gray tones for secondary text

## Typography

### Fonts
1. **Heading 1**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Bold) - Main headings, hero titles
2. **Heading 2**: Apple Garamond / EB Garamond - Subheadings, section titles
3. **Body Text**: Plus Jakarta Sans (Regular) - All body content, paragraphs, buttons

### Font Weights
- Bold (700): Main headings
- Semibold (600): Subheadings
- Regular (400): Body text

## Logo Usage
- Use the official Ambivers logo with "AMBI" in black, "VERS" in white on yellow background, ".ID" in blue
- Maintain logo proportions and spacing
- Minimum size: 120px width for web

## Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Yellow background with dark text
- Secondary: Blue background with white text
- Outlined: Border with transparent background

### Cards
- Clean white background with subtle shadow
- Rounded corners (8-12px)
- Hover effects with slight elevation

### Images
- Professional, high-quality photos
- Showing diverse Indonesian students
- Modern and aspirational feel

## Layout
- Maximum content width: 1280px
- Responsive breakpoints: mobile (< 768px), tablet (768-1024px), desktop (> 1024px)
- Generous white space
- Grid-based layouts for consistency

## Visual Style
- Modern and clean
- Energetic but professional
- Approachable for Gen Z students
- Trust-building through social proof (testimonials, statistics)
